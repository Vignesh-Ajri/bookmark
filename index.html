About Us

SnapMark is a modern bookmark manager that helps you save, organize, and access your favorite websites easily. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who loves to explore the web, SnapMark ensures that your saved links are always at your fingertips.

Our Key Features

🔖 Save Bookmarks

Easily save links for quick access and never lose an important resource again.

📂 Organize

Sort your bookmarks into custom categories for better management and ease of access.

🔍 Search

Quickly find the saved bookmarks you need using advanced search functionality.

🔄 Sync Across Devices

Access your bookmarks from anywhere, whether on mobile, tablet, or desktop.

👥 Share & Collaborate

Share your bookmarks with friends or teams and collaborate on collections easily.

🛡 Secure Storage

Keep your bookmarks safe with encrypted storage and privacy-focused features.
